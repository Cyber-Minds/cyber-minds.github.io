# Course 1 - Introduction to Cybersecurity

## Lesson 1: Introduction

### What is Cybersecurity?

Many people think about **Cybersecurity** from a very straight forward point of view, and in rare cases it is… BUT …In many cases, it is far from straightforward. It represents **different things** to **various people** in **various situations**…

- For **individuals**, Cybersecurity means that their personal data is safe.
- For **small business** owners, Cybersecurity means their credit card data is properly protected.
- For **online businesses**, Cybersecurity includes protecting servers from hackers.
- For the **government**, Cybersecurity represents protecting citizens from critical infrastructure loss and national security threats.

Cybersecurity is the way to protect servers, devices, electronic systems, and networks from **digital attacks** and **damage**. Cybersecurity involves various techniques, tools, and practices to prevent, detect, and respond to **cyber threats**.

Cybersecurity is a **fast-growing** and **dynamic field** that offers **many opportunities** and **challenges** for **professionals** and **learners**.

This course covers the **fundamentals** of Cybersecurity, exploring its **purposes,** and the true meaning. Let's dive right in!

## Lesson 3: Cybersecurity Jobs

### What are Cybersecurity Jobs?

There are a diverse range of **Cybersecurity Jobs** all over the world! They play a crucial role in keeping all devices protected and safe.

#### Popular Jobs...

- **Penetration Testers:** Legally hack systems to identify weaknesses and build enhanced protections to block hackers
- **Security Specialist:** Provide advice on cybersecurity management for an organization or company
- **Incident Responders:** Rapidly manage new issues related to cybersecurity as they rise
- **Cybersecurity Managers:** Lead and manage a team of cybersecurity professionals

**Cybersecurity Jobs** offer opportunities for **career growth** and **personal development**. In a world where cyber attacks continue to increase, cybersecurity jobs are not just careers; they are the **guardians** of our digital future!

## Lesson 4: The CIA Triad

### The CIA triad represents three core principles…

#### The 3 Principles:

- **Confidentiality:** Information is accessible by authorized users ONLY!!
- **Integrity:** Integrity ensures the accuracy and reliability of information. Nobody should be able to change the data or modify it.
- **Availability:** Availability ensures that information and resources are accessible and usableby users whenever needed!

#### Balanced Approach...

The CIA Triad helps organizations strike a **balance** between security measures. It emphasizes that security should not come at the cost of **usability**, and vice versa. These **3 principles** allow the **implementation** of security policies to protect the **data** and privacy of an individual…no matter the price.
